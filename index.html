Three Easy Rules

1) Keep Like Terms Together

Only combine terms with the same variable and power. Example: 3x and 5x can combine, but 3x and 5 cannot.

2) Balance Both Sides

For equations, whatever you do on one side, do the same on the other side to keep things equal.

3) Reverse Operations

Undo addition and subtraction first, then multiplication and division, to isolate the variable.
